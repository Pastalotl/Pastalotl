That's the "cubic date"! It's just a cubic equation with roots that form the date (in dd/mm/yy form) when subject to the inequalities. It's an overly complicated and gimmicky format, but it's only for fun - so it's unimportant. The format and inequalities likely give away that the roots are equal to the date.
