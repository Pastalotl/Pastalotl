Ynglarite

Ynglarite (working name) is my passion project that I have been developing at a non-constant pace for the past ~5 years. Currently, it's still heavily ensnared in planning. It is a fantasy adventure with elements of sci-fi. The story is split into four main installments (currently planned as hypothetical turn-based TPGs), which each centre around a group of characters which fluctuates in size.
