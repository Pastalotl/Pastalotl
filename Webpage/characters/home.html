Characters

There are many characters across the parish of Clestuard - where your journey takes place. Below is a selection of the characters that you may encounter early on in your adventure.

Nigeläs av Derp - Dedicated Defender

Nigeläs is a viking warrior from the distant past.

Kauplaire Deliarr - Showboating Sharpshooter

Kauplaire is a theatrical assassin from a town nestled in the walls of a canyon.

Ifforrem - Mad Marauder

Ifforrem is an armoured outlander from the wilderness.

Dr. Calian Zariastas - Sleepless Scientist

Calian is an overqualified researcher from a city at the base of a volcano.

Ddwgyl Kaeffep - Fluvial Fieldworker

Ddwgyl is a cartographer and druid from a valley nestled amongst tall peaks.

Vayens the Righteous - Courageous Crusader

Vayens is a former commander from a proud empire.

Silaera - Whimsical Witch

Silaera is a mysterious but kind witch, seen only mentally.

Tauré Illenia - Helpful Healer

Tauré is a quiet medic from the local village of Kemglais.

Mr Swanopoly - Ostentatious Oligarch

Mr Swanopoly is an annoying business-swan, residing in a nearby lake.

Peter - Fearful Feline

Peter is an omniscient kitten, currently visiting Clestuard.
