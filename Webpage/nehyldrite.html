About Nehyldrite

Nehyldrite is a long-time worldbuilding project made by Pastalotl. It is planned to take form as several installments of turn-based RPGs, which provide a window into some of the adventures in the world. There is currently one demo version for the first planned version, Nehyldrite: Rescindment.

The story focuses on multiple main characters, usually grouped together, from different backgrounds, cultures, species and worlds altogether. The first installment, Rescindment, focuses on six characters which have expanded backstories in its post-launch content and return for its sequel - Shards Adrift. The central theme of Nehyldrite is connection - the importance of making connections, maintaining them and the need to sever them at times.

Demo Versions

Nehyldrite currently only has one demo version for the first installment - Nehyldrite: Rescindment. This demo is currently still being worked on and consists of the "Shrouded Temple" - a lone temple in the Clestuard region once inhabited by a mysterious and malicious cult. In the demo, Nigeläs, Kauplaire and Calian descend through the temple's catacombs to uncover the truth behind the cult and rout them out, once and for all.
